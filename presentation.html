History

But by the way, where did our idea for the game design come from? 

In our group, we really like cars. We all watch shows like Top Gear, The Gran Tour or Vilebrequin. So we decided to make a project that relates to this universe. We thought about several game possibilities, but we settled on a racing game rather than an RPG where players would play as cars. Moreover, a racing game is easily imaginable with a multiplayer mode and an Artificial Intelligence mode. So it was an obvious choice for us.

What about the name of the game "Bitume Legends" and the name of the development studio "CarrEnix"? 

The name Bitume Legends is a pun on the Gameloft game, Asphalt 9 Legends. We all know this game and we decided to take a lot of inspiration from its name while integrating one of our specialties: puns. The name of our team CarrEnix, is the same type. We decided to Frenchify the name of the video game studio Square Enix. In French, this gives "Carré Enix", reduced to CarrEnix.
